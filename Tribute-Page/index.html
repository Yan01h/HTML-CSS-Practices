Gabe Logan Newell

CO-Founder and CEO of Valve Corporation


Born: November 3, 1962
in Colorado, U.S.

Also known as: Gaben


Start of his Career

Gabe Logan Newell started his Career at Microsoft in the 1980s where he would work on the first three releases of the Windows. Later on he would also lead the development on a Doom port for the Windows 92 operating system.

Valve

On August 24, 1996, Gabe left Microsoft together with another employee called Mike Harrington and together they would found the Company Valve and fund their first video game called Half-Life.

Over the years, Valve would release more big titles like Portal, Counter-Strike and more. Valve and their games became a huge success and because of this, Gabe quickly became one of the most well known and richest people in the video game industry with an in 2017 estimated net worth of over $5.5 billion.

Other achievements and information

Gabe Newell is also the Owner of a marine research organization called Inkfish to do research in the deepest regions of the ocean. He would also create a reacing team called Heart of Racing together with another Valve employee called Yahn Bernier to raise funds for children's charities.
